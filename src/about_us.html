Core Values

Simplicity

Time is your most valueable asset. Simple and elegant solutions to hard problems are the only way to protect your time and your sanity.

Diversity

Your experiences have shaped who you are, and how you see this world. No one else shares them with you, and yet they have led you to this very moment, along with thousands of others. This is diversity. Innovation precipitates when our diversity mixes with a drive to build a better world.

Security

Everything in this world is temporary. Once we accept this, we can take a step back, look at the bigger picture, and gear up for the risks ahead. We take full advantage of the opportunities, but we do so responsibly.
