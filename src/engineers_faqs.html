Peacemaker delivers the data protection solution that unlocks the full value of multi-tenant SaaS and Cloud API offerings with customers that have rigorous data security requirements. Specifically, we provide drop-in Application-Layer Encryption and Key Ownership technologies.

Peacemakr’s Policy-Based Key Lifecycle Management and Secure Key Delivery systems manage and distribute keys to authorized clients. The only thing you need to do is decide what data to protect. Then your customers can bring their own keys and security policies to protect their data in your system.

There is no standard for application-layer encryption, and in our increasingly uncertain world, there’s just not enough data security engineers to continue re-build one-offs.

With Application-Layer Encryption, we strive for data to be encrypted for as long as possible. Ideally, we encrypt the data as soon as it’s created and only decrypt it only when necessary. This requires us to encrypt and decrypt data within applications themselves.

In contrast, SSL/TLS only protects data when it is in transit, leaving data exposed everywhere else. This allows bad actors many chances to steal the data from databases, applications, in logs, proxies, or even on the disk. With Peacermakr Application-Layer encryption, the data can be protected as soon as it’s created and remains encrypted for the data’s entire lifecycle.

For example, consider a log collection system that transmits data to a log aggregator. If you only encrypt with TLS, then the logs are exposed on both the original system and in the aggregator. However, by encrypting the logs as soon as they are produced with Peacemakr’s Application-Layer Encryption, then the logs are protected in all systems. The Application-Layer Encryption enabled customers to use a SaaS provider to aggregate logs without having to trust a multi-tenant cloud.

The security of your encrypted data depends on the security and management of your keys. Building a Key Lifecycle Management and Secure and Scalable Key Delivery mechanisms are not trivial engineering tasks. Further, there’s no standard to help guide what the design should be. This not only increases the likelihood of costly mistakes, but it prohibits interoperability between different systems across the different implementations, which restricts the ability to securely collaborate with future vendors, partners, and customers.

Key Ownership enforces data ownership, even when that data is inside systems that you do not control. Encrypting data with your own keys ensures a strong sense of security in a multi-tenant environment by protecting data encrypted with your keys from malicious or negligent access. This way, components in an external system can only access your data with your explicit authorization.

All the challenges of an Application-layer Encryption system apply to a Key Ownership system, but with the added complexity of managing cross-organization authorizations.

The Key Deriver is what produces all the symmetric keys. It’s a software package that you can run on any Unix platform to your own exacting standards.

When your Key Deriver is self-hosted, the data encrypted with your keys are protected against the same threat models as protect your Key Deriver.

In order to make things easy, we host the first Key Deriver for you. Whenever you’re ready to bring your key ownership in-house you have the option to relocate your Key Deriver. You can do this relocation over and over; in fact, you can do it any time you rotate your keys.

Once a key is derived it’s encrypted and delivered to clients you explicitly authorize. Not even Peacemakr can snoop on that key material.

Peacemakr’s network provides the basis for secure collaboration.

At Peacemakr, we divide our customers into Organizations. Your account belongs to an Organization that defines an authorization scope for your keys. You can share some of the keys in your Organization with any Organization in the Peacemakr network. You can enforce ownership over your keys (and therefore your data) and end a collaboration at any time.
