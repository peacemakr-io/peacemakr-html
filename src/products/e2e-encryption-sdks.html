Open-Source E2E-Encryption SDKs

E2E-encryption keeps data encrypted for as long as possible, only decrypting it within an application when required for processing. This upgrades the threat model by forcing an adversary to reverse engineer a running process in order to steal data.

Growing engineering teams can quickly simplify their data security story with a consistent, robust, and transparent interface.

Quickly ship secure apps, and let the SDKs take care of backward compatability, cross platform portability, cross cloud operational maturity, keylifecycle management, HSM or KMS integrations, or changing security requirements.
