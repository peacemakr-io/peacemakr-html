Why is IoT Data Security Difficult?

TLS and Encryption-at-Rest are necessary data security tools, but they do not stop an adversary that breaks into your system from stealing data.

Further, IoT platforms themselves are extremely resource constrained, and required optimized code, compilers, and minimal libraries - a very difficult combination for encryption libraries.

Is TLS Broken?

TLS only provides data privacy as data moves between systems. It does not protect your data when an adversary is inside your system.

Learn more →

Is Encryption-at-Rest Broken?

Encryption-at-Rest only protect your data on physical storage media. It does not protect your data when an adversary is inside your system.

Learn more →

E2E-Encryption Protects IoT Devices

E2E-Encryption keeps your IoT's data protected for its entire lifecycle, instead of just when it moves between systems (TLS) or resides on a persistence layer (Encryption-at-Rest).
